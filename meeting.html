Context for the Board: Since our last meeting, The Practice Center has expanded its mission by creating a new corporate arm: AIdedEQ (AI-aided Emotional Intelligence). AIdedEQ: Website Walkthrough

10 min
JoYi: ED & Board President
aidedeq.com: Live Walkthrough
What is AIdedEQ? AIdedEQ stands for AI-aided Emotional Intelligence. It is a suite of tools and frameworks that infuse emotional intelligence into technology-driven experiences. Where most AI tools optimize for efficiency, AIdedEQ optimizes for connection: helping facilitators, educators, nonprofit leaders, and community builders do their deepest work with technology as a partner, not a replacement.

Website Walkthrough Guide: Section by Section

  • Mission + Vision: Our mission is to ensure that as AI transforms how we work and connect, emotional intelligence remains at the center.
  • Who We Serve: Facilitators, nonprofit leaders, community organizers, educators, and organizations navigating the AI transition who refuse to lose the human element.
  • Our Focus: Building AI-powered tools rooted in facilitation best practices: tools like the Pocket Facilitator that guide real-time group dynamics, not just content delivery.
  • How We Build: Heart-first, tech-second. Every tool starts with a human need identified through real facilitation work at TPC.
  • How to Get Involved: Join as a beta tester, become a facilitation partner, fund the mission, contribute code with purpose.
JoYi's Walkthrough Notes

Presentation Guidelines for Gabby
